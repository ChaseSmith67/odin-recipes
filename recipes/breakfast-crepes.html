Breakfast Crepes

A quick and easy recipe for a delicious breakfast. May be served with butter, sugar, jam, chocolate spread, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pinch salt

Directions

  1. Whisk together flour, milk, egg, and salt in medium bowl
  2. Heat large frying pan on medium high heat
  3. Coat the surface of the pan with melted butter
  4. Pour quarter cup of batter into the pan and tilt the pan with a circular motion so that batter coats the surface in a smooth and even layer.
  5. After two minutes, lift edge of crepe to see if browning
  6. If underside is browning, flip crepe to cook other side
  7. After two minutes, other side should also be browned
  8. Repeat steps to cook the remaining crepes
