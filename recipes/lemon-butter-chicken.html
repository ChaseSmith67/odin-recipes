Lemon Butter Chicken

These baked chicken thighs take little prep time and will be a family favorite.

With only a few ingredients, this recipe is great for a weeknight meal. Serve over noodles, rice, potatoes, or with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 4 skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
