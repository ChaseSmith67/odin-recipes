Lemon Butter Chicken

These baked chicken thighs take little prep time and will be a family favorite.

With only a few ingredients, this recipe is great for a weeknight meal. Serve over noodles, rice, potatoes, or with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 4 skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F (190C)
  2. Melt 3 tablespoons butter in microwave safe bowl
  3. Smash garlic cloves and add to butter, along with lemon juice and onion powder Stir and set aside.
  4. Season both sides of chicken with salt and pepper
  5. Heat remaining tablespoon of butter in skillet on medium high heart
  6. Brown chicken, skin side down, for 3-4 minutes
  7. Flip chicken and brush skin with lemon butter mixture
  8. Pour remaining mixture into skillet and romve from heat
  9. Bake in preheated oven until juices run clear, about 30 minutes, brushing skin with pan juices every 10 minutes.
  10. Remove from oven and plate chicken
  11. Drizzle chicken with pan juices and garnish with parsley
